The Developer

Dave is a lifelong learner and continues to develop software and work with others in helping them when possible.

A lifelong learner is a person who continues to learn new skills and competencies long after they have completed their formal education. It entails not only learning different subjects but also developing an open mind and positive mindset toward the world's dynamism.

Lifelong learners are always looking for new ways to deepen their knowledge and understanding. Lifelong learning isn't necessarily associated with your career. Many lifelong learners enroll in courses to enlighten themselves about topic areas of personal interest. While academic credentials can help you advance in your career, curiosity and a proactive attitude can create new opportunities.
